Feature Selection 
=================

The features in this data set are a processed summary of another database measuring accelerometer and gyroscope 3-axial signals, tAcc()-XYZ and tGyro()-XYZ. That data contained mean and standard deviation values over various time measurements. This summary contains the average of mean or standard deviation values for each subject and physical activity monitored. 

These signals averages for each subject/activity pair are:  
‘()-XYZ’ is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions.

tBodyAcc.std()-XYZ
tGravityAcc.mean()-XYZ
tGravityAcc.std()-XYZ
tBodyAccJerk.mean()-XYZ
tBodyAccJerk.std()-XYZ
tBodyGyro.mean()-XYZ
tBodyGyro.std()-XYZ
tBodyGyroJerk.mean()-XYZ
tBodyGyroJerk.std()-XYZ

tBodyAccMag.std()
